Joypad/joystick: 
	Left: Dpad left/Stick 1 left
	Right: Dpad right/Stick 1 right
	Jump:	First button
Keyboard: 
	Left: a or left cursor
	Right: d or right cursor
	Jump: w or cursor up or space
